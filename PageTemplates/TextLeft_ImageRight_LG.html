At Cracker Barrel, three different types of cooks work together to serve our Guests the safest, tastiest food possible. As a Backup Cook, you are responsible for baking bread and cooking a number of foods in steam kettles. To do this, you will need to organize your work area and your tasks so that you always have the right amount of product available. In addition, you must clean your area and handle food safely to ensure each Guest enjoys high quality food and has a safe dining experience. The products that you prepare play a big role in providing a Great Guest Experience.

After completing this course, you will be able to recognize your general job responsibilities, your equipment and tools and their proper use, and the proper steamline setup procedures.
