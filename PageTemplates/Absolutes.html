At Cracker Barrel, we have non-negotiable minimum standards that are known as Absolutes. These are to be executed continually and guide us to perform our daily job functions in such a way that the Guest experience is consistently positive and rewarding.

  • 10 Minute or Less Ticket Times
  • Dip Tickets Prepared Immediately

You will learn more about each of these Absolutes as you complete the Grill PAR I courses.
